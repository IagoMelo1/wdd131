Website Plan

Learning with AI & Human Mentoring

1. Site Name

Learning with AI & Human Mentoring

This name reflects the purpose of the project: to demonstrate how Artificial Intelligence tools can be integrated with the guidance of human mentors to create a hybrid and effective learning environment.

2. Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase how students and professionals can learn more effectively by combining AI resources (like tutorials, real-time feedback, and personalized study paths) with the experience of human mentors.

3. Target Audience

The target audience includes students, lifelong learners, and professionals looking to upskill, as well as educators and institutions exploring ways to integrate AI tools with traditional mentoring models.

4. Content Outline

  • Home Page: Overview of hybrid learning with AI and mentors.
  • AI in Learning: Examples of AI-driven support.
  • Human Mentoring: Case studies and testimonials.
  • Interactive Page: Demo showing how AI and mentors complement each other.
  • Contact Page: Simple HTML form for feedback or mentorship inquiries.

5. Color Schema

  • Blue (#1E90FF) – Technology, trust.
  • Gold (#FFD700) – Human warmth, mentoring.
  • Black / White – Contrast and readability.

6. Typography

  • Headings: Roboto Slab (Google Fonts)
  • Body: Open Sans (Google Fonts)
